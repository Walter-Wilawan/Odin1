matcha

Ingredients

  • matcha powder
  • bowl
  • Chasen

Instuction

  1. scoop 1 teaspoon of matcha powder into a bowl
  2. heat water to 176 degrees and pour 2 to 4 ounces over the matcha powder
  3. Use a matcha whisk, also called a Chasen, to blend the water and matcha powder; briskly whisk in a "W" or "M" pattern for around 20 seconds until the matcha has evenly dissolved
